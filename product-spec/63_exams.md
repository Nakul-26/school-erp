# 63 - Exams Module Specification

This document details the configuration of school examinations, grading rules, weightage calculations, and student mark registers.

---

## 🎯 1. Business Goals
*   Custom terms and weightages structure.
*   Secure spreadsheets marks entry grids for subject teachers.
*   Grade and report cards generation.

---

## 📋 2. Key Interface Views
*   **Assessment Grid**: Spreadsheet-like input fields for entering student marks.
*   **Grading Matrix**: Grade setups (A+, A, B, etc.) and average calculations.
*   **Report Cards**: Generate downloadable PDF cards showing overall grades and comments.
