# School ERP — Priority Roadmap

## Phase A — Revenue & Compliance (COMPLETED)
- [x] **Report Cards PDF**: High-fidelity print styles for classroom report card sheets
- [x] **Fee Receipts PDF**: High-fidelity print styles for payment invoices
- [x] **Outstanding/Dues Report**: Included under the unified Reports page
- [x] **Parent/Student Portal**: Detailed fee ledger and payment receipt history access + report card viewing/printing
- [x] **Timetable Conflicts**: Backend teacher double-booking check & section overlap warning validations

## Phase B — Communication & Operations (IN PROGRESS)
- [ ] **Circular & Notice Board**: Structured, categorized official circulars for announcements with attachment and role targets.
- [ ] **SMS / Email Alerts**: Hook automated notifications (on student attendance, fee dues reminders) to external providers.
- [ ] **Library Management**: Catalog books, track issued/returned copies, calculate due-date overdue fines.
- [ ] **Transport / Bus Routes**: Define routes and vehicles, assign students, integrate monthly transportation billing into the fees ledger.

## Phase C — Growth & Upgrades (FUTURE PLAN)
- [ ] **Online Fee Payment**: Payment gateway integration (UPI, QR, Cards) with automated invoice reconciliation.
- [ ] **Multi-Branch Support**: Access controls and branches data segregation for school chains.
- [ ] **ID Cards & Official Certificates**: Print ready templates for Student IDs, Bonafide certificates, and Transfer Certificates (TC).
- [ ] **Direct Parent-Teacher Messaging**: Built-in chat channel for direct communication.
