# 66 - Institution Settings Module Specification

This document details the configuration of school profiles, departments, academic calendar ranges, and roles permissions.

---

## 🎯 1. Business Goals
*   Central school profile configurations.
*   Safe academic year cycles rollover flags.
*   Define roles and permission variables.

---

## 📋 2. Key Interface Views
*   **School Settings**: Name, Address, Contact, logo updates.
*   **Academic Years**: Add new years, activate the target cycle, archive past directories.
*   **Departments List**: Manage CSE, Math, Science department categories and assign department heads.
