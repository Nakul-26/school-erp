# 62 - Attendance Module Specification

This document details the configuration of student daily registers, session attendance trackers, and alert warnings.

---

## 🎯 1. Business Goals
*   Mark attendance under 30 seconds for any class size.
*   Generate alerts for low attendance.
*   Provide auditing logs on edits.

---

## 📋 2. Key Interface Views
*   **Registers Panel**: Selector dropdowns for Date, Class, Section.
*   **Grid**: Lists students with Present (default check), Absent, Excused options.
*   **Reports**: Monthly percentage statistics and warning banners on dashboards.
