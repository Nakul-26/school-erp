# 12 - User Personas

To build screens that fit actual user needs, developers and coding agents must align features with these user personas.

---

## 🏫 1. Principal & School Administrator
*   **Role**: High-level administrative supervision.
*   **Primary Goals**:
    *   Monitor school-wide operations (total attendance rates, collection vs deficit fees, overall exam statistics).
    *   Approve special workflows (academic year rollovers, frozen results corrections, deactivating records).
*   **Anti-Patterns (What they NEVER do)**:
    *   They do not mark daily student attendance.
    *   They do not input subject marks.

---

## 👩‍🏫 2. Subject & Class Teacher
*   **Role**: Classroom orchestration and academic instruction.
*   **Primary Goals**:
    *   Mark student attendance in under 30 seconds.
    *   Input exam marks quickly using spreadsheet key controls.
    *   Check their weekly timetable schedule slot allocations.
    *   Write notes or upload document attachments on student files.

---

## 💰 3. School Accountant & Cashier
*   **Role**: Financial auditing and collections.
*   **Primary Goals**:
    *   Configure class fee structures and waive discounts.
    *   Search student balances, collect cash payments, and print PDF receipts instantly.
    *   Generate collections and aging reports.

---

## 👪 4. Parent / Guardian (Read-Only Portal)
*   **Role**: Student progress monitoring.
*   **Primary Goals**:
    *   Check outstanding fees and get payment reminders.
    *   View student attendance history calendars.
    *   Download student report cards and term assessments results.
